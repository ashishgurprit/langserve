# Headline Formulas: 50+ Proven Templates

## Core Principle

**"On average, five times as many people read the headline as read the body copy."** — David Ogilvy

Your headline must:
1. **Stop the scroll**: Pattern interrupt
2. **Communicate benefit**: What's in it for them?
3. **Create curiosity**: Open loop that body copy closes
4. **Be specific**: Numbers, timeframes, outcomes

---

## The Top 10 (Highest Converting)

### 1. How To [Desired Outcome]

**Template**: How to [achieve goal] [timeframe/qualifier]

**Examples**:
- How to Get 1,000 Email Subscribers in 30 Days (Even Starting from Zero)
- How to Write Landing Page Copy That Converts at 15%
- How to Onboard 100 SaaS Users a Month (With a Team of 3)

**Why It Works**: Educational promise, specific outcome, achievable timeframe

---

### 2. [Number] Ways to [Achieve Outcome]

**Template**: [Number] [Ways/Secrets/Tips/Mistakes] to [achieve goal]

**Examples**:
- 7 Conversion Mistakes Killing Your SaaS Signup Rate
- 23 Shopify Apps That Generated $50K in Extra Revenue
- 5 Client Acquisition Tactics Agencies Wish They'd Known Sooner

**Why It Works**: Sets expectations (3-minute read for 7 tips), creates curiosity, scannability

## Headline Testing Framework

### What to Test (Priority Order)

1. **Benefit vs Feature**:
   - A: "Get 30% More Signups"
   - B: "AI-Powered Landing Page Builder"
   - Winner: A (benefit) ~80% of the time

2. **Specific vs Generic**:
   - A: "Get More Traffic"
   - B: "Get 10,000 Visitors in 30 Days"
   - Winner: B (specific) ~90% of the time

3. **You vs I vs They**:
   - A: "How You Can Double Conversions"
   - B: "How I Doubled Conversions in 30 Days"
   - C: "How 5,000 Companies Doubled Conversions"
   - Winner: Depends on stage of awareness

4. **Long vs Short**:
   - A: "Get More Customers"
   - B: "Get 30% More Customers in 60 Days (Even If You're Just Starting)"
   - Winner: B (long, specific) ~70% of the time for cold traffic

5. **Question vs Statement**:
   - A: "Want More Trial Signups?"
   - B: "Get 30% More Trial Signups in 60 Days"
   - Winner: Depends on awareness (Question = Problem-Aware, Statement = Solution-Aware)

## Headline Checklist

Before publishing, check:

- [ ] **Specific**: Includes numbers, timeframes, or concrete outcomes?
- [ ] **Benefit-driven**: Focuses on what reader gets (not what you do)?
- [ ] **Credible**: Promise is believable (not "Make $1M in 1 week")?
- [ ] **Audience-relevant**: Speaks to target audience's needs?
- [ ] **Curiosity gap**: Creates desire to read more?
- [ ] **Clear**: Immediately understandable (no jargon)?
- [ ] **Urgent**: Includes reason to act now (time, scarcity, consequence)?
- [ ] **Testable**: Can compare against alternative?

## Bonus: Subheadline Formulas

**Purpose**: Subheadline supports headline with detail or handles objection

### Support Headline

**Pattern**: [Headline benefit] + [How/qualifier]

**Example**:
- Headline: "Get 30% More Trial Signups in 60 Days"
- Subhead: "AI-powered landing page builder with proven templates. No code required, no designer needed."

### Handle Objection

**Pattern**: [Headline promise] + [Objection handled]

**Example**:
- Headline: "Double Your Email Open Rates"
- Subhead: "Without changing your subject lines or increasing send frequency"

### Add Proof

**Pattern**: [Headline promise] + [Social proof]

**Example**:
- Headline: "The Email Template That Lands Agency Clients"
- Subhead: "Used by 5,000+ agencies to close $120M in deals"
