# Landing Page Structure Template

## The Conversion-Optimized Blueprint

This template follows the proven structure used by Fortune 100 companies and high-converting SaaS, E-commerce, and Agency landing pages.

---

## Section 1: Above-the-Fold (Hero)

**Purpose**: Capture attention, communicate value, direct to action within 3 seconds

### Components

**1. Headline** (H1)
- **Pattern**: [Desired Outcome] in [Timeframe] / [Benefit Statement]
- **Length**: 5-12 words
- **Focus**: Benefit (what they get), not feature (what you do)

**Examples by Industry**:
```
SaaS:
"Get 30% More Trial Signups in 60 Days"
"Turn Website Visitors into Paying Customers. Automatically."

E-commerce:
"Luxury Sneakers That Don't Sacrifice Comfort"
"30-Day Free Returns. Always."

Agency:
"Land 5 Retainer Clients in 90 Days"
"Done-For-You SEO That Actually Generates Leads"
```

---

**2. Subheadline** (H2 or large paragraph)
- **Pattern**: [How/What makes it unique] + [Objection handling]
- **Length**: 10-25 words
- **Focus**: Add specificity or handle biggest objection

**Examples**:
```
SaaS:
"AI-powered landing page builder with 50+ proven templates.
No code required, no designer needed."

E-commerce:
"Engineered with memory foam and breathable mesh.
All-day comfort for people who walk 10,000+ steps."

Agency:
"We handle everything: strategy, content, link building.
You just approve and watch leads come in."
```

---

**3. Primary CTA Button**
- **Copy Pattern**: [Action] + [Benefit] (first-person)
- **Design**: High contrast, large (minimum 200×50px)
- **Placement**: Right side or center, above fold

**Examples**:
```
SaaS:
"Start My Free 14-Day Trial →"
"Get My Custom Report"
"Book a Free Demo"

E-commerce:
"Shop Now - Free Shipping"
"Add to Cart"
"Get 20% Off Today"

Agency:
"Get My Free Strategy Call"
"Download Our Case Study"
"See How We Can Help"
```

---

**4. Hero Image/Video**
- **SaaS**: Product screenshot, dashboard in action, or explainer video
- **E-commerce**: Product photo (high-quality, lifestyle context)
- **Agency**: Before/after results, client logos, or team photo

**Best Practices**:
- Show product in use (not abstract stock photos)
- Include faces when possible (humans connect with humans)
- Optimize for speed (< 500KB for images, lazy load video)

---

**5. Trust Signals**
- **Pattern**: [Customer count] + [Rating/Review] + [Logos]

**Examples**:
```
"Trusted by 12,000+ SaaS companies"
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4.9/5 on G2 (from 2,400+ reviews)
[Logo] [Logo] [Logo] [Logo] [Logo]
```

## Section 2: Social Proof

**Purpose**: Build credibility, overcome skepticism

### Components

**1. Customer Logos** (If B2B)
- 5-10 recognizable brands
- High-quality logos (PNG, transparent background)
- Gray scale or brand colors (consistent styling)

```
As Seen On / Trusted By / Used By

[Nike Logo] [Spotify Logo] [Airbnb Logo] [Stripe Logo] [Shopify Logo]
```

---

**2. Testimonials** (3-5 featured)

**Structure**:
```
"[Specific result quote]"

— [Full Name]
  [Title] at [Company]
  [Photo]
```

**Example (SaaS)**:
```
"We doubled our trial-to-paid conversion rate in 30 days.
The ROI was 10x in the first month alone."

— Sarah Johnson
  VP of Growth at TechStartup
  [Professional headshot photo]
```

**Best Practices**:
- Specific results > Generic praise ("10x ROI" > "Great product!")
- Include photo (95% more credible)
- Include full name + title + company (not just initials)
- Use real quotes (don't fabricate)

---

**3. Stats** (Big Numbers)

**Pattern**: [Impressive number] + [Metric]

**Examples**:
```
$500M+
Revenue Generated for Clients

47,000+
Active Customers

99.9%
Uptime SLA
```

## Section 3: Problem/Solution (PAS Framework)

**Purpose**: Amplify pain, position solution

### Structure

**Problem** (2-3 sentences):
```
You're spending 10 hours a week manually following up with leads.
Your inbox is chaos. Hot leads go cold because you forgot to reply.
```

**Agitate** (2-3 sentences):
```
Every day you delay, you're losing money. Your competitor just
responded in 5 minutes while you're still sorting through emails.
That $50K deal? Just went to them.
```

**Solution** (2-3 sentences + CTA):
```
[Product] automates your entire follow-up sequence. Set it up
once, and every lead gets a personalized message at exactly the
right time. Your close rate doubles while you spend zero time
managing spreadsheets.

→ [CTA Button: "Start Free Trial"]
```

## Section 4: Features/Benefits Grid

**Purpose**: Show what you do (features) and why it matters (benefits)

### Structure (3-Column Layout)

**Pattern**: [Icon] + [Benefit Headline] + [Feature Description]

```
[Icon: Lightning Bolt]
Set Up in 5 Minutes
Connect your CRM, choose a template, and launch.
No IT team required. No technical skills needed.

[Icon: Target]
Personalized for Each Visitor
Show the right message to the right person at the right time.
Conversions increase by 3x on average.

[Icon: Chart]
A/B Test Everything
Test headlines, CTAs, images—everything. Know what works,
double down on winners. No guesswork.
```

**Best Practices**:
- Lead with benefit headline (what they get)
- Support with feature (how it works)
- Keep to 3-6 items (more = overwhelm)
- Use icons for scannability

---

## Section 5: How It Works (3-Step Process)

**Purpose**: Show it's easy, reduce friction

### Structure

```
How It Works

[Step 1 Icon/Number]
Connect Your Tools (2 minutes)
Link your CRM, email platform, and analytics.

[Step 2 Icon/Number]
Choose a Template (1 minute)
Pick from 50+ proven high-converting templates.

[Step 3 Icon/Number]
Launch & Optimize (Ongoing)
Go live in 3 minutes, then A/B test to continuously improve.
```

**Best Practices**:
- 3 steps (not 2, not 4)
- Include time estimates (shows speed)
- Use action verbs (Connect, Choose, Launch)
- Visual progression (arrows or timeline)

## Section 6: Objection Handling (FAQ)

**Purpose**: Address hesitations before they become blockers

### Structure

**Common Objections by Industry**:

**SaaS**:
```
Q: Is it hard to set up?
A: Most customers are live in under 10 minutes. Plus, we have
   24/7 live chat if you get stuck.

Q: Does it integrate with [tool]?
A: Yes! We integrate with 100+ tools including Salesforce, HubSpot,
   Stripe, and Zapier. See full list →

Q: What if I need to cancel?
A: Cancel anytime with one click. No contracts, no cancellation fees,
   no questions asked.

Q: Is my data secure?
A: Yes. SOC 2 Type II certified, GDPR compliant, and encrypted at rest
   and in transit. Your data is never shared.
```

**E-commerce**:
```
Q: What's your return policy?
A: 30-day free returns. No questions asked. We even cover return shipping.

Q: How long does shipping take?
A: 2-3 business days in the US, 5-7 days international. Free shipping
   on orders over $50.

Q: Are these true to size?
A: Yes! 95% of customers say they fit true to size. Check our size
   guide for detailed measurements.
```

**Agency**:
```
Q: How long before I see results?
A: Most clients see measurable results within 60-90 days. We provide
   monthly reports so you can track progress.

Q: Do you guarantee results?
A: We don't make guarantees (no ethical agency does), but we do
   guarantee our effort. If we don't hit milestones, we work for
   free until we do.

Q: What if I'm not happy?
A: We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not satisfied
   in the first month, we'll refund you in full.
```

## Section 7: Pricing (If Applicable)

**Purpose**: Make decision easy, show value

### Structure (3-Tier Pricing)

```
[Starter]               [Growth]                  [Enterprise]
$49/month              $99/month ← MOST POPULAR  Custom Pricing

For solo founders      For growing teams         For large orgs
• Feature 1            • Everything in Starter   • Everything in Growth
• Feature 2            • Feature A               • Feature X
• Feature 3            • Feature B               • Feature Y
                       • Feature C               • Dedicated support

[CTA: Start Free]      [CTA: Start Free]         [CTA: Contact Sales]
```

**Best Practices**:
- Show 3 tiers (people pick middle 60% of the time)
- Highlight recommended plan (visual badge: "MOST POPULAR")
- Annual discount (e.g., "Save 20% with annual billing")
- Feature comparison (what's included in each)
- Money-back guarantee below pricing

**Purpose**: Last chance to convert, remove friction

### Structure

```
[Large Section with Background Color]

Ready to [Achieve Outcome]?

[Large Benefit-Driven CTA Button]
Start Your Free 14-Day Trial →

No credit card required. Cancel anytime.
Join 12,000+ companies growing with [Product].

[Trust Badge] [Security Badge] [Money-Back Badge]
```

**Best Practices**:
- High contrast background (stand out)
- Repeat primary CTA (same copy as above-fold)
- Risk reversal (no credit card, money-back guarantee)
- Social proof reminder (customer count)

## Mobile Optimization Checklist

Landing pages must work perfectly on mobile (60%+ of traffic):

- [ ] **Headline**: Readable at 24-28px (mobile)
- [ ] **CTA buttons**: Minimum 44×44px (thumb-friendly)
- [ ] **Images**: Optimized for mobile (< 300KB)
- [ ] **Forms**: Single column, large inputs
- [ ] **Spacing**: Generous padding (prevent mis-taps)
- [ ] **Load speed**: < 3 seconds on 3G

---

## A/B Testing Priority

Test in this order (highest impact first):

1. **Headline** (80% of success)
2. **CTA button copy** (action vs benefit)
3. **Hero image** (product vs lifestyle vs video)
4. **Social proof placement** (above vs below fold)
5. **Pricing** (monthly vs annual default)
6. **Form length** (short vs long)

---

## Industry-Specific Variations

### SaaS Landing Page
- Focus: Trial signups or demo bookings
- Must-Have: Product screenshot, pricing, integrations
- Social Proof: Customer logos, G2 rating, user count

### E-commerce Landing Page
- Focus: Product purchases, AOV
- Must-Have: High-quality images, size guide, shipping info
- Social Proof: Reviews, ratings, "X people bought this"

### Agency Landing Page
- Focus: Lead generation (consultation bookings)
- Must-Have: Case studies, results, process
- Social Proof: Client logos, testimonials, portfolio

---

**Remember**: This template is a starting point. Test everything. What works for one audience may not work for another. Let data guide your decisions.
