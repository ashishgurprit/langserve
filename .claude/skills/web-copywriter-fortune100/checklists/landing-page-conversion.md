# Landing Page Conversion Checklist

## Pre-Launch Checklist (50+ Items)

Use this checklist before launching ANY landing page to maximize conversion rate.

---

## Section 1: Above-the-Fold (Critical)

### Headline

- [ ] **Benefit-driven** (what they get, not what you do)
- [ ] **Specific** (includes numbers, timeframes, or concrete outcomes)
- [ ] **Under 12 words** (readable in 3 seconds)
- [ ] **Immediately understandable** (no jargon, no ambiguity)
- [ ] **Matches ad/email copy** (if traffic is coming from campaigns)
- [ ] **Addresses target audience** (speaks to their needs)

**Test**: Can a 12-year-old understand the value in 3 seconds?

---

### Subheadline

- [ ] **Supports headline** (adds detail or handles objection)
- [ ] **Under 25 words** (one sentence max)
- [ ] **Includes qualifier** (e.g., "No code required" handles tech objection)
- [ ] **Not redundant** (doesn't repeat headline verbatim)

---

### Primary CTA Button

- [ ] **Action-oriented** (starts with verb: Start, Get, See)
- [ ] **Benefit-included** (e.g., "Start Free Trial" not just "Submit")
- [ ] **First-person** ("Start My Trial" beats "Start Your Trial" by 90%)
- [ ] **High contrast** (stands out from background)
- [ ] **Large enough** (minimum 200Ã—50px, 44Ã—44px for mobile)
- [ ] **Above the fold** (visible without scrolling)
- [ ] **White space around it** (draws eye naturally)
- [ ] **No competing CTAs** (one primary action above fold)

---

### Hero Image/Video

- [ ] **Shows product in use** (not abstract stock photos)
- [ ] **High quality** (not blurry, pixelated, or amateur)
- [ ] **Optimized for speed** (< 500KB for images, lazy load for video)
- [ ] **Includes faces** (humans connect with humans)
- [ ] **Relevant to audience** (they can see themselves in it)
- [ ] **Has alt text** (for accessibility + SEO)

---

### Trust Signals

- [ ] **Customer count visible** (e.g., "Trusted by 12,000+ companies")
- [ ] **Ratings/reviews** (e.g., "4.9/5 stars on G2")
- [ ] **Recognizable logos** (5-10 brand logos if B2B)
- [ ] **Security badges** (SSL, GDPR, SOC 2 if applicable)
- [ ] **Placed near CTA** (builds trust before action)

---

## Section 2: Social Proof

### Testimonials

- [ ] **Specific results** (not generic praise like "Great product!")
- [ ] **Includes photo** (95% more credible than text alone)
- [ ] **Full name + title + company** (not just initials)
- [ ] **3-5 testimonials** (more = decision fatigue)
- [ ] **Strategically placed** (after benefits, before pricing)
- [ ] **Diverse** (different industries, company sizes, use cases)
- [ ] **Recent** (dated within 12 months)

**Bad**: "Amazing product! - J.D."

---

### Customer Logos

- [ ] **5-10 recognizable brands** (more = cluttered)
- [ ] **High-quality** (PNG, transparent background)
- [ ] **Consistent styling** (all grayscale OR all color)
- [ ] **Relevant to audience** (B2B shows enterprise, B2C shows consumers)
- [ ] **Permission obtained** (legally allowed to use logos)

---

### Stats/Numbers

- [ ] **Specific numbers** ("47,000" > "Thousands")
- [ ] **Relevant metrics** (users, revenue, time saved, etc.)
- [ ] **Verifiable** (can prove if asked)
- [ ] **Large and visible** (use big fonts, standout design)

---

## Section 3: Copy & Messaging

### Value Proposition

- [ ] **Clear within 5 seconds** (what you do, who for, why better)
- [ ] **Differentiated** (not "we're better," but specifically how)
- [ ] **Customer language** (words they use, not marketing jargon)
- [ ] **Outcome-focused** (transformation, not just features)

---

### Body Copy

- [ ] **Scannable** (short paragraphs, 2-3 sentences max)
- [ ] **Bullet points** (for lists, features, benefits)
- [ ] **Subheadings** (break up text, guide eye)
- [ ] **Active voice** ("We help you..." not "You will be helped...")
- [ ] **No typos** (proofread 3+ times)
- [ ] **Readable font** (minimum 16px, high contrast)
- [ ] **Benefits before features** (always lead with "what they get")

---

### Objection Handling

- [ ] **Top 3-5 objections addressed** (price, complexity, time, trust, fit)
- [ ] **FAQ section** (common questions answered)
- [ ] **Risk reversal** (money-back guarantee, free trial, no credit card)
- [ ] **Placed strategically** (before final CTA)

**Common Objections by Industry**:
- SaaS: "Is it hard to set up?" "Does it integrate with [X]?"
- E-commerce: "What's the return policy?" "How long does shipping take?"
- Agency: "How long before results?" "What if I'm not happy?"

---

## Section 4: Features/Benefits

### Benefit Headlines

- [ ] **Lead with benefit** (what they get)
- [ ] **Support with feature** (how it works)
- [ ] **3-6 items** (more = overwhelm)
- [ ] **Icon for each** (visual scannability)
- [ ] **Consistent format** (all same structure)

**Bad**: "We have AI-powered analytics"
**Good**: "See exactly where users drop off (AI-powered analytics)"

---

## Section 5: CTAs (Throughout Page)

### Primary CTA (Top)

- [ ] **Above the fold** (no scrolling required)
- [ ] **Repeats 2-3 times** (top, middle, bottom of page)
- [ ] **Same copy everywhere** (consistency)
- [ ] **High contrast** (stands out visually)

---

### Secondary CTAs (Optional)

- [ ] **Lower commitment** (e.g., "Watch Demo" vs "Start Trial")
- [ ] **Different color** (visually distinct from primary)
- [ ] **Doesn't compete** (primary CTA still dominant)

---

### Microcopy (Below Button)

- [ ] **Reassurance** ("No credit card required")
- [ ] **Social proof** ("Join 12,000+ companies")
- [ ] **Time estimate** ("Takes 60 seconds")
- [ ] **Risk reversal** ("Cancel anytime")

---

## Section 6: Pricing (If Shown)

### Pricing Display

- [ ] **3 tiers** (people pick middle 60% of time)
- [ ] **Recommended plan highlighted** (visual badge)
- [ ] **Annual discount shown** (e.g., "Save 20%")
- [ ] **Feature comparison** (what's included in each)
- [ ] **Money-back guarantee** (below pricing)
- [ ] **No hidden fees** (transparent pricing)

---

### Pricing Copy

- [ ] **Value-based** (not just price, but what you get)
- [ ] **Clear differentiators** (why pay more for higher tier?)
- [ ] **CTA for each tier** (easy to start)

---

## Section 7: Forms (If Applicable)

### Form Design

- [ ] **Short as possible** (only essential fields)
- [ ] **Single column** (easier to scan)
- [ ] **Large inputs** (minimum 44px height for mobile)
- [ ] **Clear labels** (above inputs, not placeholder-only)
- [ ] **Logical order** (name, email, phone, etc.)
- [ ] **Error messages** (specific, helpful, near field)
- [ ] **Success state** (clear confirmation)

---

### Form Copy

- [ ] **CTA button** (action-oriented, benefit-driven)
- [ ] **Privacy note** (GDPR compliance if EU traffic)
- [ ] **No required fields marked** (asterisk + text "Required")

**Best Practice**: Every field you remove = 10% more conversions

---

## Section 8: Technical (UX & Performance)

### Page Speed

- [ ] **Load time < 3 seconds** (on 3G)
- [ ] **Images optimized** (WebP/AVIF, < 500KB each)
- [ ] **Lazy loading** (for images below fold)
- [ ] **Minified CSS/JS** (remove unused code)
- [ ] **CDN for assets** (faster global delivery)

**Test**: Google PageSpeed Insights, GTmetrix

---

### Mobile Optimization

- [ ] **Responsive design** (works on all screen sizes)
- [ ] **Readable text** (minimum 16px font on mobile)
- [ ] **Touch-friendly** (buttons 44Ã—44px minimum)
- [ ] **No horizontal scroll** (content fits screen)
- [ ] **Fast mobile load** (< 3 seconds on 4G)
- [ ] **Mobile CTAs visible** (above fold)

**Test**: Chrome DevTools mobile emulator

---

### Accessibility

- [ ] **High contrast** (WCAG AA: 4.5:1 text, 3:1 large text)
- [ ] **Alt text for images** (descriptive, not "image123")
- [ ] **Keyboard navigable** (can tab through entire page)
- [ ] **Focus states visible** (clear outline on buttons/inputs)
- [ ] **Semantic HTML** (proper heading hierarchy H1 â†’ H2 â†’ H3)
- [ ] **Screen reader friendly** (test with NVDA/VoiceOver)

---

### Cross-Browser

- [ ] **Works in Chrome** (70% of traffic)
- [ ] **Works in Safari** (15% of traffic)
- [ ] **Works in Firefox** (8% of traffic)
- [ ] **Works in Edge** (5% of traffic)

---

## Section 9: Trust & Credibility

### Security & Privacy

- [ ] **SSL certificate** (HTTPS, lock icon)
- [ ] **Privacy policy link** (footer)
- [ ] **Terms of service link** (footer)
- [ ] **GDPR compliant** (if EU traffic)
- [ ] **Security badges** (if applicable: SOC 2, ISO, etc.)

---

### Contact Information

- [ ] **Email visible** (footer or contact page)
- [ ] **Physical address** (if B2B, builds trust)
- [ ] **Phone number** (if applicable)
- [ ] **Live chat** (if available, increases conversion)

---

## Section 10: Conversion Tracking

### Analytics

- [ ] **Google Analytics** (or equivalent) installed
- [ ] **Goal tracking set up** (for conversions)
- [ ] **Event tracking** (button clicks, scroll depth)
- [ ] **Heatmap tool** (Hotjar, Crazy Egg, Microsoft Clarity)
- [ ] **Session recordings** (see how users navigate)

---

### A/B Testing

- [ ] **Testing tool installed** (Google Optimize, VWO, Optimizely)
- [ ] **Hypothesis documented** ("Changing headline will increase signups by 10%")
- [ ] **Statistical significance** (wait for 95% confidence, ~350 conversions per variant)
- [ ] **One variable at a time** (don't test headline + CTA simultaneously)

---

## Section 11: Industry-Specific Checks

### SaaS

- [ ] **Free trial CTA** (no credit card required)
- [ ] **Product screenshot** (show dashboard/interface)
- [ ] **Integrations listed** (top 5-10 tools)
- [ ] **Pricing transparent** (no "Contact for pricing")
- [ ] **Demo video** (optional but helpful)

---

### E-commerce

- [ ] **High-quality product images** (5+ angles)
- [ ] **Zoom functionality** (see details)
- [ ] **Size guide** (if applicable)
- [ ] **Shipping information** (time + cost)
- [ ] **Return policy** (clear, upfront)
- [ ] **Reviews/ratings** (star rating + count)
- [ ] **"Add to cart" CTA** (not "Buy now")
- [ ] **Trust badges** (secure checkout, money-back)

---

### Agency

- [ ] **Case studies** (3-5 with results)
- [ ] **Client testimonials** (with photos)
- [ ] **Portfolio** (examples of work)
- [ ] **Process** ("How we work" section)
- [ ] **Team photos** (builds trust)
- [ ] **Free consultation CTA** (low commitment)

---

## Section 12: Legal & Compliance

### FTC Compliance (US)

- [ ] **Truthful claims** (can prove all claims)
- [ ] **Typical results disclosed** (if showing testimonials)
- [ ] **Affiliate disclosure** (if applicable)
- [ ] **Clear pricing** (no hidden fees)

---

### GDPR Compliance (EU)

- [ ] **Cookie consent banner** (if tracking)
- [ ] **Privacy policy** (clear data usage)
- [ ] **Opt-in email** (no pre-checked boxes)
- [ ] **Easy unsubscribe** (for emails)

---

### CAN-SPAM (Email)

- [ ] **Accurate sender** (From name matches sender)
- [ ] **Physical address** (in email footer)
- [ ] **One-click unsubscribe** (easy opt-out)

---

## Final Pre-Launch Checks

### Quality Assurance

- [ ] **Proofread 3+ times** (no typos, grammar errors)
- [ ] **Links work** (all CTAs, nav, footer links)
- [ ] **Forms submit** (test actual submission)
- [ ] **Confirmation email** (if form submitted)
- [ ] **Thank you page** (post-submission)
- [ ] **404 page** (custom, not default)

---

### Pre-Launch Review

- [ ] **Show to colleague** (fresh eyes catch mistakes)
- [ ] **Test on real device** (mobile phone, not just emulator)
- [ ] **Clear browser cache** (see what new visitors see)
- [ ] **Check in incognito** (no cookies, clean slate)

---

## Post-Launch Monitoring (First 7 Days)

### Metrics to Watch

- [ ] **Conversion rate** (track daily)
- [ ] **Bounce rate** (< 40% is good)
- [ ] **Time on page** (> 90 seconds is engaged)
- [ ] **Scroll depth** (% reaching bottom)
- [ ] **Heatmap** (where are they clicking?)
- [ ] **Session recordings** (watch 20+ sessions)

---

### Quick Wins (If Conversion Is Low)

1. **Change headline** (test benefit vs current)
2. **Simplify CTA** (remove friction words)
3. **Add social proof** (above fold)
4. **Remove fields** (shorter form = more conversions)
5. **Speed up page** (< 3 seconds load time)
